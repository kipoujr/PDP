You are given N (at most 500.000) positive integers (each at most 1.000.000.000).
Your task is to find the biggest set of consecutive numbers whose GCD is more than 1.

Example:
Input
-----
7
11 3 6 8 4 17 2

Output
------
3

Explanation:
[6 8 4] have GCD 2.
There is no other set of 3 consecutive numbers having GCD more than 1.
